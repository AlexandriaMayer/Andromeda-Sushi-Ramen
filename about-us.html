The Day the World Changed

In the sumemr of 2000, WPD psychic detective Dave Pattersoft was enjoying our establishment, when he sensed shapeshifters. He took notice - but was unable to reveal the hidden identies at the time. A week later, he returned to our restaurant, and noticed the same shapeshifters were here, eating plateloads of sushi. For the record, the shapeshifters were good customers, and tipped well. He ended up unveiling the shapeshifters, and what was revealed were not from Earth or Guf.
